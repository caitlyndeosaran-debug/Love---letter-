For You

After last night’s call, I had to type this out. I guess you could call it a love letter because honestly, if I tried to say all of this out loud, I probably would’ve bawled my eyes out. So, to prevent that, here it is in words.

What I love most about you is how emotionally mature you are. You always seem to know when something is off with me, even when I don’t notice it myself. With you, I never feel like I have to put on a front or always be this strong, independent girl. You make it easy for me to let my guard down, lean on you, and just be myself, and that’s something so rare.

You’ve become my biggest support system. You never doubt me, you always believe in me, even when I struggle to believe in myself. You’re honest, maybe a little too honest sometimes, but you never sugarcoat things. You tell me what I need to hear, when I need to hear it, and I admire you so much for that. You push me to grow, to stop being lazy, and to reach my limits. And for that, I’ll always be grateful, because you inspire me to be better every single day.

It means so much that you never make me feel like I’m too much or not enough. You listen, you remember the little things I say, and you somehow find ways to make even the smallest, ordinary moments feel so special. You make me laugh until my cheeks hurt, and somehow, you make me feel safe through a screen. You’re my favourite notification to get, and you always know how to balance being serious with making me laugh at exactly the right time. Around you, I never have to worry, you make it easy for me to be me

Even with the distance between us, you find ways to keep me fulfilled and loved endlessly. Your kisses, your fashion shows, and your rants well, not really rants, but your rants about cricket and cars, make me fall for you even more, you silly goose You’re so cute, and a little mean and bossy sometimes, but truly one of a kind…….my pineapple.

At the end of the day, what matters most is that being with you feels like home, no matter how far apart we may be.

I’m sorry if I ever made you doubt the love I have for you. Please know that it’s always there, it’s pure, genuine, and I hope you can feel it as much as I do.
